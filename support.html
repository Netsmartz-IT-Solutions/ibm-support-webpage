Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is IBM watsonx Orchestrate?

IBM watsonx Orchestrate is an AI-powered automation platform that helps organizations streamline business processes by combining AI, automation, and human collaboration. It allows you to deploy and manage AI agents that assist with various business tasks.

Q: How do I access the HR AI Agents?

All three agents (JD Generator, Questionnaire Generator, and Resume Scoring) are available directly in your IBM watsonx Orchestrate workspace under your available agents. Simply click on the agent you need to launch it. No installation required.

Q: What's the difference between the three agents?

JD Generator: Creates and refines job descriptions. Questionnaire Generator: Builds interview question sets with scoring rubrics. Resume Scoring: Evaluates candidate resumes against job requirements.

Q: Can I use all three agents together in my hiring workflow?

Absolutely! They're designed to work together: Start with JD Generator to create job descriptions, use Questionnaire Generator to build interview questions, and use Resume Scoring to evaluate incoming applications.

Q: What information do I need to provide to each agent?

JD Generator: Job title and experience level (minimum). Questionnaire Generator: Role, experience, skill focus. Resume Scoring: Job description text and resume(s).

Q: Do the agents work for both technical and non-technical roles?

Yes! All three agents are designed to work across all types of roles and industries, from software engineers to marketing managers, data scientists to HR specialists.

Q: Are the agents bias-aware and compliant with fair hiring practices?

Yes. All agents are designed to avoid discriminatory content and follow responsible hiring practices. They do not use protected characteristics (age, gender, race, religion, etc.) in their evaluations or outputs.

Q: Can the Resume Scoring agent compare multiple candidates?

Yes! The Resume Scoring agent supports multi-candidate evaluation. You can submit multiple resumes and receive individual scorecards plus a comparison summary with rankings.

Q: Does the Questionnaire Generator support emerging tech skills like GenAI?

Absolutely. The agent supports questions for emerging areas including GenAI, LLMs, MLOps, Responsible AI, and prompt engineering, in addition to traditional technical and behavioral topics.

Q: How do I get access to the JD Generator?

The JD Generator is available as a partner solution on IBM watsonx Orchestrate. Contact your IBM watsonx Orchestrate administrator or Netsmartz support team to enable access to the agent in your workspace.

Q: Is my data secure when using the agent?

Yes. The JD Generator operates within IBM watsonx Orchestrate's secure environment and follows IBM's security and privacy standards. All data is processed according to IBM's enterprise-grade security protocols.

Q: Does the agent make hiring decisions or evaluate candidates?

No. The JD Generator focuses solely on creating and refining job description content. It does not evaluate candidates, make hiring recommendations, or participate in applicant screening.

Q: What if the agent doesn't understand my request?

The agent will ask clarifying questions when necessary information is missing. If you receive unexpected output, try rephrasing your request or providing more specific details about the role.

Q: Can I use the same agent for multiple job descriptions?

Yes! You can create as many job descriptions as you need. Each request is handled independently, allowing you to generate JDs for different roles in the same session.

Q: Are there any usage limits or restrictions?

Usage limits depend on your IBM watsonx Orchestrate subscription plan. Contact Netsmartz support or your IBM account manager for specific details about your plan's limitations.

Known Limitations

All Agents

  • Do not provide legal, compliance, or regulatory advice
  • Will not generate biased, discriminatory, or exclusionary content
  • Do not access external databases, ATS systems, or proprietary company data
  • Work only with information provided in the conversation

JD Generator

  • Company-specific policies, benefits, and compensation must be added manually
  • Uses generic placeholders that require customization
  • Does not fabricate certifications or requirements not provided by user

Questionnaire Generator

  • Does not claim access to internal policies or proprietary job descriptions
  • Cannot integrate with interview scheduling or ATS platforms
  • Questions are templates that may need role-specific adjustment

Resume Scoring Agent

  • Privacy-first: does not repeat personal identifiers (phone, email, DOB, addresses) in outputs
  • Ignores protected attributes and does not use them in evaluation
  • Provides decision support only‚Äîfinal hiring decisions remain with humans

Quick Troubleshooting

Agent is not responding

Solution: Check your IBM watsonx Orchestrate connection. Try refreshing your workspace or logging out and back in. If the issue persists, contact support.

Output is not what I expected

Solution: Provide more specific details and context. All agents work best with clear, detailed input. You can ask any agent to refine or modify specific sections of their output.

Agent asks too many questions

Solution: Provide comprehensive initial input with all relevant details upfront. This reduces the need for clarifying questions.

Cannot find the agents in my workspace

Solution: Verify that your organization has access to the HR AI Agents and that they have been enabled for your workspace. Check with your IBM watsonx Orchestrate administrator or contact Netsmartz support for access assistance.
